Our Comprehensive Services


CargoCraft Services offers a full spectrum of shipping and logistics solutions tailored to meet your

unique needs. We ensure efficiency, security, and timely delivery for every shipment.

Express Parcel Delivery

Fast and reliable delivery for urgent packages. Our express service ensures your shipments reach their destination in the shortest possible time, with end-to-end tracking.

  • Domestic & International
  • Time-sensitive Shipments
  • Door-to-door service

Warehousing & Distribution

Scalable warehousing solutions with efficient inventory management and streamlined distribution networks. Optimize your supply chain with our state-of-the-art facilities.

  • Secure Storage Facilities
  • Inventory Management
  • Order Fulfillment

Road Freight

Flexible and cost-effective road transportation for both full truckload (FTL) and less-than-truckload (LTL) shipments across vast networks.

  • Full Truckload (FTL)
  • Less-Than-Truckload (LTL)
  • Regional & Long Haul

Air Freight

Speedy and efficient air cargo services for urgent and high-value shipments. We handle customs clearance and ensure seamless transitions from origin to destination.

  • Global Coverage
  • Expedited Options
  • Customs Clearance

Ocean Freight

Reliable and economical ocean freight services for large volume shipments. Full Container Load (FCL) and Less than Container Load (LCL) options available.

  • Full Container Load (FCL)
  • Less than Container Load (LCL)
  • Port-to-Port & Door-to-Door

Customs Brokerage

Expert customs brokerage services to navigate complex international trade regulations. We ensure compliance and smooth clearance for your imports and exports.

  • Import & Export Declarations
  • Duty & Tax Calculation
  • Regulatory Compliance
